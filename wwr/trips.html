Who Are We?

We are the White Water Adventures, a once in a lifetime sporting service. Our mission is to provide a unforgettable experience to our rafters. We have been providing family fun and excitement to those who dare to cross the New river.

Where Are We Located?

We are located in the New River Gorge National Park. Want to know how to get there? Visit our Contact Us page.

What Should I Bring?

You may want to wear a swimsuit, water shoes, and sunscreen for you will get wet and be out in the sun for several hours. If you are doing the full day, lunch will be provided. Please bring something that will protect your electronics whilst you are out on the river, for you may tip and lose said electronics if they are not protect.
